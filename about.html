About FitTrack

Our Mission

FitTrack was founded in 2025 by a group of health-conscious students who recognized the need for a comprehensive wellness platform tailored specifically for university life.

Our mission is to empower students to take control of their health by providing easy-to-use tracking tools, educational resources, and a supportive community.

We believe that small, consistent changes lead to big results, and we're here to help you every step of the way on your wellness journey.

What We Offer

FitTrack provides a complete suite of tools to help students maintain a healthy lifestyle:

  • Activity tracking with progress visualization
  • Nutrition logging and calorie tracking
  • Wellness event calendar and registration
  • Educational resources and articles
  • Community support and challenges
